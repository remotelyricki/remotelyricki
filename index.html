About Me

Welcome to my page! My name is Ricki, and I am an aspiring web developer. I will be using this portfolio as a way to document my progress in learning to code one lesson at a time.

I have some experience coding doing the University of Central Florida Coding Bootcamp, but I wanted to expand on that knowledge and master the lessons that I did not retain.

My hope is that at the end of this journey I will have a portfolio full of projects that I have completed and documentation of everthing that I learned. This will serve both as a reference for myself and other coders, and will showcase everything that I know for future employers to see.
